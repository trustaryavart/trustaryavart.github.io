About Us

Aryavarta Siddhanta Sanrakshaka Trust is a social organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Sanatan Dharma, Sanskrit, Culture, and Sanskar. Established under the Indian Trust Act 1882, our mission is to revive and share the timeless wisdom of Rishi-Munis for the welfare of humanity.

Our goal is to make India a global leader in spiritual, cultural, and intellectual domains, reclaiming our ancient glory.
